10 Years Of Grace

The history of JOS Society at NMU is a remarkable journey of faith, vision, and growth. Founded a decade ago by Pastor W Sibande during his first year as an undergraduate, JOS began as a humble gathering of a few students—never with the intention of leading a church, but simply following a calling to share the Word, as he had done back home. What started in a small study room at XM Xanadu soon blossomed into a vibrant community, moving from the dining hall at XM Xanadu to the largest lecture venue on campus, Building 45: Room 40, now welcoming over 400 students. Through the years, JOS Society has become the most recognized and celebrated society at NMU, earning the title of number one society for consecutive years. Even the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic could not halt its momentum—because Jesus has always been the solution and the foundation. This 10th anniversary is not just a celebration of years, but a testimony to God’s grace, faithfulness, and the unwavering trust of its members. From small beginnings to great impact, JOS Society stands as a beacon of hope and unity, inspiring generations to come.
