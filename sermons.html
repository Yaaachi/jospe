Our Teachings

Our teachings mostly come in series of episodes, helping you to gain deep insight into the Word of God. Each series is carefully crafted to address different aspects of Christian living, spiritual growth, and practical faith. Through systematic study, we encourage believers to not only hear the Word but also to apply it in their daily lives. The importance of sound teaching is emphasized throughout Scripture. In 2 Timothy 3:16-17 (NIV), it is written: "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work." This highlights that biblical teaching is foundational for equipping believers to live out their faith. Jesus Himself taught in series and parables, guiding His disciples step by step (Matthew 13:34-35). By following this model, our teachings aim to build understanding over time, allowing for deeper reflection and transformation. As Romans 10:17 says, "So then faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God." We invite you to join us on this journey, exploring the richness of God's Word together, one episode at a time.

Praise & Worship

Our worship is designed to accommodate every language a believer might speak, creating an inclusive environment where everyone can participate fully. By embracing multilingual worship, we ensure that students and members from different nations feel at home and valued. This approach not only breaks down language barriers but also celebrates the diversity within our congregation, allowing each person to connect with God in a way that is meaningful to them. As a result, students from various backgrounds experience a sense of belonging and unity, making our community a true reflection of God's love for all people.
